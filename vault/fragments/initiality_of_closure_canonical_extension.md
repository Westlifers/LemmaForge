# Conditional initiality of the closure construction

Assume that \(\mathcal Q\) admits the exactness needed for the closure construction of \(\mathcal A^\delta\), and assume that the compactness condition is stable under the relevant weighted limits and weighted colimits. Then the resulting pair \((\mathcal A^\delta,Y_{\mathcal A})\) is initial among \(\mathcal Q\)-canonical extensions of \(\mathcal A\). More precisely, for every \(\mathcal Q\)-canonical extension \((\mathcal B,\eta')\) of \(\mathcal A\), there is a unique complete \(\mathcal Q\)-functor \(F:\mathcal A^\delta\to\mathcal B\) such that \(F\circ Y_{\mathcal A}=\eta'\).
