# Possible Phi-canonical refinement of compactness

The finite-witness compactness condition may be too strong for general quantaloids. A weaker version might require compactness only for weights generated by representables, or for weights belonging to a chosen class \(\Phi\). In that case one should speak of a \(\Phi\)-canonical extension rather than an unrestricted \(\mathcal Q\)-canonical extension.
