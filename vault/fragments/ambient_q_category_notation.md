# Ambient quantaloid and presheaf notation

Let \(\mathcal Q\) be a small quantaloid and let \(\mathcal A\) be a separated \(\mathcal Q\)-category. Write \(P\mathcal A\) for the \(\mathcal Q\)-category of presheaves on \(\mathcal A\), and write \(Y_{\mathcal A}:\mathcal A\to P\mathcal A\) for the Yoneda embedding.
